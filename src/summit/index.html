A Gathering

The Interledger Community

We couldn’t think of a better place to host our vibrant, global Interledger Community to connect, showcase, and celebrate our vital work in the financial inclusion and payments space. We’re enthused to see folks IRL, learn from each other, and share a preview of the groundbreaking roadmap that lies ahead for the Interledger Protocol.

Over the course of two days you’ll experience keynote speeches by industry leaders, participate in engaging workshops & sessions, and enjoy all the The Big Easy has to offer!

Call for Papers

Travel Grants

Submissions Closed 8/30

Too often, the opportunity to travel to conferences like the ILP Summit are only available to a privileged few. At the Interledger Foundation we wanted to ensure a diverse range of voices are in the room as we discuss shaping financial inclusion through open protocols. Therefore, we are excited to award 20 Travel Grants to members of the Interledger community, with a preference given to individuals applying from developing nations (please see list of countries that qualifies as Developing by the World Bank.) Each awardee received up to a $2,500 travel award for travel expenses and incidentals.
