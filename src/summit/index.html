#ILPSummit2022

The Interledger Summit is back! Save the date, we're bringing everyone to New Orleans for the latest edition of the Interledger Summit. Expect a weekend full of fun, learning, and hacking on the Interledger ecosystem!

The Interledger Summit brings together developers, contributors and entrepreneurs to learn, build, and celebrate all things Interledger.

Connect and collaborate with community members from projects including Coil, Fynbos, Grant for the Web, Rafiki and Web Monetization.

The Summit is a free event with a strong in-person component, that will be partially live-streamed. Tickets for the in-person aspect of the event will go live soon, save the date and check back on the website for ticket applications.
