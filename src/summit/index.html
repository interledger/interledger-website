Hey Friends! Now that outside is open again, we're excited to announce we're bringing back the Interledger Summit this year in Nawlins, y’all! (translation: New Orleans, you all). The Interledger Community

We couldn’t think of a better place to host our vibrant, global Interledger Community to connect, showcase, and celebrate our vital work in the financial inclusion and payments space. We’re enthused to see folks IRL, learn from each other, and share a preview of the groundbreaking roadmap that lies ahead for the Interledger Protocol.

Over the course of two days you’ll experience keynote speeches by industry leaders, participate in engaging workshops & sessions, and enjoy all the The Big Easy has to offer!
