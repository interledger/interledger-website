Join the Interledger Foundation

There are so many ways you can help the Interledger Foundation achieve its mission of connecting everyone to their futures. Your support will help the Foundation expand its education, community programming and global reach.

We are seeking to build a diverse group of members from financial institutions, tech companies, academia, web advocacy and open standards enthusiasts to help shape the future of The Interledger Foundation, Interledger Protocol and its community programming.

Members will work with The Interledger Foundation to:

  • Broaden global awareness of the mission
  • Encourage adoption of Interledger Protocol
  • Contribute to community programming
  • Maintain, build and oversee new technology solutions
